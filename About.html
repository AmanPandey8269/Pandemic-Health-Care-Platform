    • Know more about the site

    This website is created to give people all the necessary Health care services from the comfort of there home. You can order medicines online, consult a doctor, fix an appointment with a specialist or at any hospital, Book Vaccination slot or get vaccinated at home. We tried to give every Healthcare facility we can. You can contact us for assitance by chat or on call from the website itself.

    We designed the Website not to be to complex and so User friendly so that anyone who can read and understand english can easily navigate through the website but We have also implemented a feature to get service through call for the people who find it hard to navigate through the website.

    Our Vision

    Our goal is to help as much people as we can during the time of any emergency or any other Pandemic. We saw what happened during Covid-19, many people suffered due to lack of resources and many casualties occurred. So we decieded to take a step forward and build a platform where people can get all the help they need during times like those be it Searching for Hospitals Nearby or get Medicines delivered at there home and many more things. We wanted to get ready for anything that comes next and the start of it was by making this website.

    About the Developers

    The minds behind this website are of Aman Pandey, Vishal Jain, Shivam Shivhare and Saurabh Kushwah. They got the idea for making this website during the Corona pendamic. At that time many people were not able to get necessities like blood, oxygen cylinder and so on. They had to look everywhere so hard for getting these all things. They decided to create a all in one website where people could find all the necessary things at a single place. This is how the idea of this website was born and took form with the help of RGPV hackathon.
